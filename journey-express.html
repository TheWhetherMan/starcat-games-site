# Journey Express

## About The Game


Discoveries

Find and catalog unique discoveries around the world, and use them to fund future journeys!

Exploration

Explore the lands around you at any time while traveling to gather supplies, fish and hunt for food, or to find hidden points of interest!

Traveling

Travel through grasslands, forests, deserts, mountains, and tundras while braving the ever-changing weather!

Character Customization

Customize your travel guide with different portraits, backgrounds, and traits to make each journey unique! If your character doesn't manage to survive the journey, your family successor will pick up where the last left off!
